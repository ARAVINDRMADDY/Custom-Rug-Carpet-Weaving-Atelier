The WeaveNest Approach

The Art of Creation

Every masterpiece begins with a vision. Our meticulous four-step process ensures your custom rug is a flawless reflection of your personal aesthetic.

Phase 01

Design Consultation

We collaborate with you to define patterns, colors, and dimensions that harmonize with your space.

Phase 02

Material & Dyeing

Selecting premium wool or silk, hand-dyed using natural pigments to achieve the exact tonal palette.

Phase 03

Hand-Knotting

Master artisans spend months bringing the design to life on traditional wooden looms, knot by knot.

Phase 04

Finishing

Washing, stretching, and hand-clipping the pile to ensure perfection before final delivery to your home.
