Our Process

The Bespoke Journey

Commissioning a rug is an intimate collaboration. We guide you from initial concept to the final, masterful installation in your space.

01

Consultation

We discuss your vision, review floor plans, and select the ideal color palettes and textures for your home.

02

Design & Render

Our designers create a true-to-scale digital CAD rendering and produce physical strike-offs (yarn samples) for approval.

03

The Weaving

Master artisans hand-dye the wool and spend 3 to 6 months intricately knotting your rug on a traditional wooden loom.

04

Final Delivery

After washing, stretching, and hand-carving the pile, your finished masterpiece is shipped and placed in your home.
