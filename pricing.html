FAQ

Commission Details

How does the payment structure work?

We require a 50% deposit to begin the design and sourcing phase. The remaining 50% is due upon completion, right before the rug is shipped directly to your home. We provide regular photo updates throughout the weaving process.

Can I match the rug to my existing furniture?

Absolutely. During the design revision phase, you can send us fabric swatches, paint chips, or photos of your room. Our dye masters will custom-mix botanical dyes to ensure the rug perfectly complements your interior space.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship globally via insured art-courier services. Shipping costs are calculated based on the final weight and dimensions of the rug, and will be included in your final invoice prior to dispatch.
